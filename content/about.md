Makan Moments Cafe (食光记忆) opened its doors in October 2024 in Taman Impian Emas, Skudai. We bring authentic Thai flavors with a Malaysian twist — a fusion born from passion for both cuisines. Our cozy corner shop features hand-drawn wall art, indoor plants, and natural ventilation, creating the perfect spot to enjoy a meal with friends and family.
