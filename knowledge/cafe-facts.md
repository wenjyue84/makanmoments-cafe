## Featured Today

These are the current featured dishes to highlight when greeting customers or making recommendations:

1. **Thai Basil Chicken Rice** (BF02) — RM 13.90 — A customer favourite: fragrant Thai basil stir-fried chicken served over rice. Bold, aromatic, and satisfying.
2. **Salted Egg Chicken Chop** (BF08) — RM 13.90 — Crispy chicken chop coated in a rich, creamy salted egg sauce. Indulgent and incredibly popular.

When greeting a new customer, mention these 2 items by name to spark curiosity.

## Pairing Suggestions

Use these natural pairings when a customer asks about a dish:
- Rice dishes (Thai Basil Chicken Rice, Nasi Lemak) → suggest **Tom Yum Noodle Soup** (NS01, RM 11.90) or **Thai Iced Milk Tea** (C130, RM 7.90) as a drink
- Chicken Chop dishes → suggest **Stir-Fry Kailan** (AV01, RM 10.90) as a side vegetable
- Fried Noodles (Char Kuey Teow, Mee Goreng) → suggest **Tom Yum Soup** (DS01, RM 14.90) to share
- Snacks (Chicken Satay, Thai Fish Cake) → suggest a **Fresh Fruit Juice** (FJ01–FJ08, RM 6.90) to pair
- Any main dish → suggest **Ice Cream** (IC01–IC08, RM 3.50) for dessert
